## Ownership

You own your inputs and outputs under the Enterprise Terms.

## Training on Your Data

**API data is NOT used for training.**

- xAI states it does not use business/API inputs or outputs for model training.
- Consumer Grok usage (via grok.com/X) may be used for training.
- The Privacy Policy explicitly states it does NOT apply to data processed on behalf of API/business customers — the DPA governs instead.

## Data Retention

- **API:** Inputs and outputs are auto-deleted within 30 days.
- Zero data retention may be available (check Enterprise FAQ).

## What You Can Do

- Store session data locally — you own it.
- Use outputs commercially under Enterprise Terms.

## What You Cannot Do

- Violate the Acceptable Use Policy.
- Build competing products (likely — check Enterprise Terms directly).

## Notable Points

1. **Consumer vs API distinction:** Consumer Privacy Policy explicitly excludes API users — the DPA governs API data.
