1. consumer-terms.md
   Source: https://www.anthropic.com/legal/consumer-terms
   Effective: October 8, 2025
   Purpose: Terms of Service for individual users on Free, Pro, and Max plans.
   Relevance: Governs Claude Code usage when authenticated via OAuth (personal account).
   Key points for session storage:
   - You own your inputs and outputs.
   - Anthropic may train on your data unless you opt out.
   - Retention: 5 years (opt-in) or 30 days (opt-out).

2. commercial-terms.md
   Source: https://www.anthropic.com/legal/commercial-terms
   Effective: June 17, 2025
   Purpose: Terms of Service for Team, Enterprise, and API key users.
   Relevance: Governs Claude Code usage when authenticated via API key or organizational account.
   Key points for session storage:
   - You own your inputs and outputs (explicit commercial use rights).
   - Anthropic cannot train on your data.
   - Retention: 30 days (or zero with ZDR).

3. acceptable-use-policy.md
   Source: https://www.anthropic.com/legal/aup
   Effective: September 15, 2025
   Purpose: Restrictions on what you may use Claude services for.
   Relevance: Applies to all users regardless of plan. Constrains what session
   data may contain or be used for (no illegal activity, weapons, CSAM, fraud,
   building competing AI models, etc.).

4. privacy-policy.md
   Source: https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy
   Effective: January 12, 2026
   Purpose: How Anthropic collects, uses, and shares personal information.
   Relevance: Covers what personal data Anthropic retains from sessions,
   how it is processed, and your rights regarding that data (access, deletion, etc.).

5. claude-code-legal-and-compliance.md
   Source: https://code.claude.com/docs/en/legal-and-compliance
   Purpose: Claude Code-specific legal guidance.
   Relevance: Explains which set of terms applies based on authentication method

   Also covers BAA/healthcare compliance and OAuth token restrictions.

6. claude-code-data-usage.md
